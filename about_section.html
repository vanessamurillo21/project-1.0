About Us

Established Since 2020

Started prior to the pandemic of 2020, we always wanted to utilize our creativity to turn nothing into something. What started as small pieces for special occasions like birthdays, annivesaries, holidays evenutally turned into something we wanted devout our attention too. As any business, trial and error was and is something we often face but it allows to grow. We believe that our gifts we given to us by God and we seek to honor him with this blessings we recieved. All glory always belongs to Him and we are always forever thankful for enabling us to be where we are at today. We love putting a smile on the faces of our customers and bringing their ideas to life which allows them to express the unique style they seek to express. We understand the importance of durability and longevity in jewelry so we prioritize having tarnish-resistance materials. Overall, we thank you for taking the time to look over our business.
